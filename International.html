International Funds Transfers via SWIFT 

(11 Ratings)
Now you can transfer money worldwide via SWIFT on ACLEDA mobile conveniently. Please see how to transfer here.

Since your business is growing from day to day, and fund transfer has played an important role in your global business, we have innovated a new way to enhance our products and services quality delivering to you. Recognising the need for its customers to lower the cost of transferring money from end to end, ACLEDA Bank registered with an international level financial message transfer service — SWIFT.

Receive money transfer in all currencies sent from worldwide via SWIFT and free of charge. This offer is valid until the end of 2023. Terms & conditions apply.
